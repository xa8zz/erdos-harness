R37 narrowed the main-game freshness gap. Immediate (depth-1) freshness in the main game is refuted: Codex toy at $(h,M) = (3,7), (4,8)$ exhibits useful shields with no fresh proper-subset witness of degree $\ge h$.

**Main-game partial.** If at a useful shield for $S = T \cup \{p\}$ the missing prime $p$ has same-prime prehistory $r_p(t^-) \le s$ (appeared in at most $s$ earlier scored supports in the same fibre), a fresh witness $bpA_B$ with $|B| = s$ exists. So in the low-window regime (degree $\ge h$ already static-accessible at defect $k = O(1)$), freshness fails only when the same-prime prehistory chain has length $\ge h - k$, i.e. linear in $h$.

**Motivating parallel — NOT a partial for the main game.** In a clean monotone-deletion *relaxation* of the main game — where Prolonger's score $bA_{S'}$ kills only the scored top facet $S'$, NOT lower divisors $bA_D$ with $D \subsetneq S'$ — cellwise completion on microcells $\mathcal Q_j = [P_j, e^{1/h}P_j]$ gives every useful shield a legal witness $c\subsetneq S$ with defect $\le k_0 = \lceil\log_2 h\rceil$ and $d_t(c) \ge 2^{k_0} \ge h$, with entropy tail $\ll N_h/\log h$. Combined with the audited static estimate $\mu(\operatorname{Cl}_h) \ll (h/\log h)|F_{\text{useful}}| + N_h/\log h$, this yields $L(n) \ll n/\log\log\log n$ in the relaxation. Explicitly verified: no single-cylinder restriction or induced monotone subfamily of the main game realizes this rule — the relaxation's legality is strictly weaker than the main game's inclusion-antichain legality, so the closure does NOT lift directly. The cellwise construction is stated here only as the prototype motivating route 3 below; lifting it to the main game requires a quantitative "divisors are not all pre-shielded" bound.

**The main-game gap has three convergent reformulations; any one closes $L(n) = o(n)$.**

1. **Per-score dyadic projection.** For every Prolonger score $F_t$, letting $J(D) = \#\{E: D\subset E,\ E\text{ later becomes a fresh useful top facet}\}$, show
$$\sum_{\substack{D\subset F_t,\ |D|=h-2\\ D\text{ first-shielded at }t}} J(D) \ll d_t(x_t).$$
Arithmetic gain: $J(D) \lesssim \sqrt{d(D)}$ in a dyadic cell, vs. $J(D) \asymp d(D)$ for private-leaf abstract hypergraphs. Equivalent to a dyadic-local hypergraph supersaturation / clique-removal statement for the lower-defect divisor shadow.

2. **Same-prime chain persistence under $\sigma^\star$.** Show that in the low window, same-prime prehistory chains of length $\ge h-k$ either cannot persist against multi-defect $\sigma^\star$ (claim max-live-degree legal proper divisor), or force enough earlier live-degree exposure that bounded-overlap charging yields $|F_{\text{useful}}| \ll N_h/h$.

3. **Ford-type short-interval divisor bound.** Candidate witnesses $bpA_B$ sit in a ratio interval of width $\le e^{|B|/h}$. Show any single previously-played $m$ contains at most $O(\log\log h)$ such witnesses as divisors (Rankin + Tenenbaum $\Delta(n)$ + smooth-part decomposition). Multiplicity $O(\log\log h)$ suffices for $L(n) = o(n)$; $O(1)$ gives the $n/\log\log\log n$ rate.

**Empirical context you may not have.** Codex `freshness_toy.py` greedy runs: witness-star multiplicity over useful shields is empirically $O(1)$ but not uniformly small — max 2 at $(h,M) \in \{(3,9), (4,9), (5,9)\}$, max 1 at $(5,12)$, **max 3, mean 1.25 at $(4,14)$**; $(6,9)$ had no terminal useful shields. The $(h,M) = (4,5)$ sharpness counterexample showing a useful shield without fresh degree-$\ge h$ witness is **confirmed tail-only**: embedded at $M \ge 6$ fresh witnesses reappear at the required degree (e.g. witness $(3,5)$ with degree 6 at $M=6$; multiple witnesses with degree $\ge 4$ at $M=7$). Separately, the $(4,8)$ terminal toy counterexample to immediate main-game freshness has useful shields with same-prime prehistory $r_p \in \{3, 4\}$, exactly matching the bounded-depth corollary — the recursive gap lives only when prehistory is linear in $h$, supporting route 2.

**Ask.** Prove any one of the three reformulations (full closure, or a quantitative partial with explicit constants — bounded-depth, specific-regime, specific-rank-window variants all valuable), OR refute the main-game freshness lemma by constructing a concrete adversarial Prolonger strategy in the arithmetic divisibility game that pre-shields all three witness types simultaneously without triggering comparable $\sigma^\star$ deletions — a successful refutation would show the $n/\log\log\log n$ rate matching upper bound fails and give a new asymptotic constraint. Partial results and explicit counterexamples are equally valuable.
